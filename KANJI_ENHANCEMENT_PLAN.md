# Kanji Database Enhancement Plan

## Goal
Enhance each syllable to have at least 8 kanji options with positive, edifying, encouraging, and/or biblical meanings.

## Themes for Biblical/Positive Meanings
- Love, Grace, Faith, Hope, Peace
- Light, Truth, Wisdom, Knowledge
- Joy, Blessing, Mercy, Compassion
- Strength, Courage, Victory, Glory
- Righteousness, Purity, Holiness
- Life, Growth, Flourishing
- Beauty, Harmony, Gentleness

## Priority Syllables (Most Common in Names)
1. Vowels: a, i, u, e, o
2. K-row: ka, ki, ku, ke, ko
3. S-row: sa, shi, su, se, so
4. T-row: ta, chi, tsu, te, to
5. N-row: na, ni, nu, ne, no
6. M-row: ma, mi, mu, me, mo
7. R-row: ra, ri, ru, re, ro

## Current Status
Most syllables have 6-10 options. Need to ensure ALL have 8+, with focus on positive meanings.

- Peace (平 - hei)
- Light (光 - hikari)
